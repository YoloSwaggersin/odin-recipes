Stuffed Bell Peppers

Baked Stuffed Bell Peppers with Rice and Beef

In this dish, we'll be removing the top of each bell pepper to stuff it. Before this, we need to cook
what we'll be stuffing it with. After browning your ground beef (or honestly whatever protein you want)
and cooking up your rice, you can mix them together with some cheese and fill each decapitated pepper
with this and some shredded cheese. You can also top it with some mozzarella if you want to make a big
impact. After lining them up on a baking tray with some water, we set them in the oven to melt the
cheese and cook the peppers, which also lets the flavor of the meat disperse. When you're done, it's a
good idea to garnish with some greens to bring out the other colors.

In my opinion, this is a very easy recipe for any first timers. It teaches you the fundamentals of a
lot of dishes. Browning some meat and cooking some rice are fundamentals, and teaching that something
as simple as cheese can bring a dish together is something that shouldn't be understated. On top of
all of that, it's really good! The possibilities for experimentation are wide, go nuts.

  • shredded cheese
