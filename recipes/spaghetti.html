Spaghetti with Tomato Sauce

This minimal recipe from me is really only two ingredients.
I used to make it a lot for my girlfriend, a vegetarian. I
usually like some ground beef in my spaghetti, so usually
when eating this I'm missing something. Guess I gotta improve!

Ingredients

  • spaghetti pasta
  • tomato sauce
  • water

Numbered Steps

  1. boil water
  2. place noodles in, follow time on box
  3. while boiling noodles, put sauce on low heat
  4. when finished with noodles, mix and enjoy
